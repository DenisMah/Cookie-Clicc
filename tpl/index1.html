Our Story

International Burch Bakery was founded in 2021 by a group of enthusiastic professors and students at International Burch University. What started as a casual baking club quickly turned into a passion project that combined education and culinary delight. The idea was simple: if we could teach complex theories and solve equations, why not bake the best cookies in the world?
